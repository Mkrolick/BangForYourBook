Direct: Student-to-student transactions
Convenient: No shipping hassles
Affordable: No external fees or taxes
TextbookConnect is a textbook marketplace for students to buy and sell their textbooks directly within their campus.

Guide:

1. Create an account.

2. Browse books in “Browse” or find your required books in “Classes”.

3. Click on a textbook to find a listing or create a listing!

4. Contact your textbook buddy to finish the transaction.

5. Save money!
